Passwords

Passwords are the essentail part of protecting your information. If you are using a weak password you could be opening yourself up to unnessacery attacks. Follow these steps to build a strong password.

At least one uppercase
At least three numbers
At least one symbol
At least twelve characters

Example: HelloWorld827$

Network

Network security is essintal because these networks can leave you vulnerable to a hacker

Try not to use Public Networks

Be mindful of your personal information on Public Networks

Do not let untrusted people on a personal network

Malware

Malware can cause for you to lose your personal infomariton, be spyed on, and destroy your computer system. You should...

Only download products from approved sites

Use Virus Detection software

Keep backups of your personal data

Cover your computers camera and microphone

Social Engineering

Social enginnering is a problem because it takes advantage of people for trusting others

Make sure you are on the right website

Do not look at spam emails

Do not trust sketchy websites

Do not answer personal questions that could be linked to your security questions
